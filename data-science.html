Data Science

Data science is a field of applying cutting-edge analytics methods and scientific concepts to data for the extraction of useful information that may be used for strategic planning, business decision-making, and other purposes.

  • Last Update : August 23, 2019
  • Category : Web Design

Description

Are you curious to learn more about data science but unsure of where to begin? This course will give you the fundamental knowledge and abilities that every data scientist needs to get started in the field or enhance their education.

You will learn about data science and what data scientists perform through this specialty. You'll gain knowledge of numerous open source tools and data science applications used by data scientists, as well as concepts like statistical analysis and relational databases.

What You will Learn?

  • Learn how to use common data science tools like GitHub.
  • Get into the mentality of a data scientist and use an approach to attack various data science problems.
  • Describe data science, its uses and use cases, and the numerous jobs that data scientists carry out.
